My Skills

Teamwork

  • Teamwork involves being able to work well with others, even if you don't agree with them.
  • It's important to put your personal feelings aside and focus on the task.
  • Working effectively within a team often requires taking direction from those in charge and being a team player.

Empathy

  • Empathy is the ability to understand and share the feelings of another person.
  • It's important to consider situations from someone else's perspective and be sensitive to their feelings.
  • Those who are empathetic are typically skilled at resolving conflicts and building relationships.

Understanding body language

  • Body language is the way we use our bodies to communicate.
  • It's important to be aware of your own body language and how it's coming across to others.
  • Those who are good at understanding body language are typically better at communication and building relationships.
